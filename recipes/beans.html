Puerto Rican Beans

Beans recipe from Puerto Rico that is great with white rice and either red or white meat. Recipe is a family one that has been passed down from my Grandparents, to my mother and then to me.

Ingredients:

  • Pink Beans
  • Sofrito
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sazon
  • Jamon (Ham bouillon, powder or cubes)
  • Adobo
  • Garlic Powder
  • Oregano

Steps:

  1. on medium heat place 1 tablespoon of oil in a pot (either Corn, Veg, Canola, Olive)
  2. 2 Tablespoons of Sofrito
  3. 3 Tablespoons of Tomato Sauce
  4. 1 packet of Sazon
  5. 1 packet/cube of Jamon (Ham bouillon)
  6. 2 Tablespoons of Adobo
  7. 2 Tablespoons of Garlic Powder
  8. 2 Tablespoons of Oregano
  9. Mix ingredients and let simmer
  10. Open bean can and remove included liquid
  11. poor beans into pot and add half a can of water
  12. when simmer, lower heat to low-medium and stir frequently for 20 minutes.
